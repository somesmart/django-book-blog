The Geek Quotient shows the variety of novels I read, as measured by the uniqueness of the world they are set in (compared to the real world) combined with the reality of the events in the book. Lower values are more realistic and are set in a world more like our own. A Geek Quotient of 0 would be a novel set in our world, and consisting only of completely realistic events, while a score of 20 would be the opposite.

This chart shows a spread of the all the books read by geek quotient, as defined above. Rather than stricly comparing books by genre, this gives a better sense of which books fall together in terms of their realism, and the uniqueness of their world.
